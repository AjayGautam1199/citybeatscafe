Our Story

From humble beginnings to Nagpur's beloved cafe

Young Entrepreneur
Starting His Dream
üì∏ Story Image

It all started in 2018 when Ayush Bhandarkar, a young man from a modest family in Nagpur, had a dream bigger than his circumstances. With just ‚Çπ25,000 saved from his part-time job as a delivery boy, Ayush envisioned creating a space where people could find comfort, community, and exceptional coffee.

Growing up in a small apartment with his mother, a domestic worker, and his younger sister, Ayush understood the value of hard work and perseverance. He spent months studying coffee brewing techniques, learning recipes from YouTube, and saving every rupee to make his dream a reality.

The first City Beat's Cafe was a tiny 200 sq ft space near Ring Road. With second-hand furniture, a basic coffee machine, and an unshakeable belief in his vision, Ayush opened the doors to what would become Nagpur's most beloved cafe.

Today, City Beat's Cafe serves over 500 customers daily, employs 15 people from the local community, and has become a symbol of hope for dreamers across Maharashtra. Our success story proves that with determination, quality, and heart, even the smallest beginnings can create the biggest impact.

"Every cup we serve carries the love and dreams of our journey. Welcome to our family!" - Ayush Bhandarkar, Founder
